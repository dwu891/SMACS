Exploring Your Universe

Sunday November 17, 2013

12 PM - 5 PM

UCLA Court of Sciences






Exploring Your Universe is an annual event featuring a myriad of activities and demonstrations open to the public to encourage interest in science fields. Activities for all age groups are available as well as planetarium shows, grad student talks, solar telescope viewing, comet making, weather tours, dinosaur fossils and more!


SMACS is looking for volunteers to man our booth and conduct demos for EYU attendees. If interested please contact Samuel Chiang at sam.b.chiang@gmail.com.
