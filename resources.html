Learning Tools
www.OnlineCourses.com
  • OnlineCourses.com is a great resource for students to obtain supplemental materials to complement their courses. OnlineCourses offers students a database of open courseware from the world's leading universities, is updated yearly and is freely available to students.
  • Check out the Chemistry and Science sections for materials that may pertain to your courses.
Organic Chemistry:
  • Voodoo - full of tips and resources for organic chemists of many levels
  • AIST:RIO-DB Spectral Database for Organic Compounds,SDBS - popular database for literature IR, NMR, etc.
  • Chemorphesis - learn organic chemistry through illustrated comic book-style stories, metaphors, and explanations of important concepts
Inorganic Chemistry:
  • Inorganic Mechanics - includes animations
