Green Chemistry Activities

SMACS is delving into uncharted waters this year in an effort to promote awareness about Green Chemistry. There are many exciting events taking place this year to promote awareness of green chemistry which you can be a part of:


  • Earth Day Fair 2014

  • Faculty Presentations/Guest Speakers

  • Environmental Film Viewings (ie: An Inconvenient Truth)

  • Field Trips!!!

  • Green Chemistry T-Shirt Designs


If interested, please contact Grace Woo at gracethechemist@yahoo.com.
