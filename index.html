SMACS Week 2 Announcements
April 8, 2014
Thanks to everyone who came to General Meeting I today! Here's a list of all the other happenings going on this quarter:

UCLA Bruin Day: Saturday April 19 8am-2pm

Every year, SMACS volunteers at the Chemistry & Biochemistry Dept booth, helping with welcoming and recruiting new students and transfers to UCLA. It’s a great opportunity to tell incoming students what UCLA has to offer and what amazing experiences you have had thus far. If interested, please sign up for a shift by clicking here: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/1OEUlT8WqwuHhpzVsF0QkbGd617VYmM7V6PpSjas8FGQ/viewform


General Meeting 2: SMACS and Professionals

Tuesday, April 22nd at 6:30PM in YH2033

SMACS is collaborating with Teach for America to bring you a panel of professionals (professors, doctors, pharmacists, professionals in industry) to come talk about their story and their thoughts on STEM Education. Come with your questions and an open mind as you are in the process of deciding what to do with your science degree!


SMACS at the 2014 Earth Day Fair

Want to learn more about the chemistry of your personal care products (e.g. cosmetics, shampoo, deodorant, etc) while also teaching others about less toxic alternatives and making an all-natural, vegan moisturizer? Get involved with the Earth Day Fair on Wed, April 23rd!! Volunteers are needed to represent SMACS at the fair between 10 am-2 pm (shifts based on your availability), as well as for set-up and clean up. We also need helpers to make the poster board and the homemade moisturizer. If you are interested in helping in any (or all!) of these areas, fill out the participation form here: https://docs.google.com/forms/d/12zy0xSidvShwIpXD5lsJvsbrgmlIIE2O5uLDyquVU5k/viewform


UCLA Cogeneration Plant Tour

This quarter, we will be attending a tour of UCLA's very own natural gas power plant. It's called a "Co-gen plant" because it combines heat and power production, making it twice as efficient as a normal power plant. The tour will be hosted by Ms. Nurit Katz, UCLA's chief sustainability officer. This is definitely an opportunity you don't want to miss!! More details and sign-ups to be announced at the upcoming meeting.

SCALACS Women Chemists Committee Dinner Meeting

ACS Younger Chemists Committee Ice Cream Social

Get Involved With SMACS This Quarter!
February 1, 2014

Happy Lunar New Year! As we head into the second half of the quarter SMACS is gearing up with more events and activities!


General Meeting 2

Join us for a presentation by Dr. Merchant of the UCLA Department of Chemistry on his exciting research in "Genomic Approaches to Studying Photosynthesis".


Chemistry Bowl

This year SMACS will be competeting with other local ACS student chapters in Southern California in a chemistry showdown. The competition will include spectroscopy, lab bench challenges (ie: titration and battery building), chemistry-themed relay obstacle course, and chemistry jeopardy (for three finalist teams). Content will be limited to general and organic chemistry.

SMACS is looking to build a team of five talented students to participate in this competetition. Students of all levels are invited to apply.

Please fill out this Interest Form if you are interested in participating.
